I watched the worst person in the world today



I cried so much. not even cause of the film itself, but because the whole time I couldn’t stop thinking about you. when it ended, I was left with this overwhelming emptiness, and I still can’t seem to find my place. I think the film captures something terrifyingly real how easy it is to lose someone truly precious while you’re still trying to figure yourself out. and that realization scared me. I’m scared of losing our future. I’m scared of losing you. I don’t want to wake up one day and realize I made the same mistakes as Julie the kind of mistakes you can never fully take back. maybe that’s why this film affected me so deeply

I really want you to watch it too. I’d love to know what you think and how it makes you feel. I have a feeling we’d spend hours talking about it, the way only we ever could. I regret everything that happened between us. I regret my words, my reactions, the way I handled things. I feel like only now I’m beginning to understand how much pain I caused you, and how much pain I caused us. If I could go back there are so many things I would do differently

I know that you’re the only person who’s ever truly been this important to me. despite everything. and that’s exactly why this hurts so much. I never wanted to be the reason you suffered. I never wanted to become someone who made the person I love feel alone. even as I’m writing this I’m crying and I can’t stop. maybe because for the first time in a long while I’m no longer trying to run away from what I feel. I’m just letting myself feel it. and what I feel is so much love for you, so much longing, and so much regret




***“Your voice has stayed in my head. We still have great conversations”***




your voice still lives in my head. sometimes I catch myself imagining what you’d say, what joke you’d make how we’d end up debating a film or talking about philosophy until one of us fell asleep. somehow, our conversations never really stopped. they still happen in my mind every single day. I miss you more than I know how to put into words. right now more than anything I just wish I could hold you. no arguments no trying to prove who’s right or wrong. just hold you and feel that warmth between us again. I don’t know what our future looks like but I know one thing with absolute certainty I don’t want to lose you because of fear pride or the mistakes we’ve made. you were never just random person in my life. you became part of who I am part of my thoughts part of the way I see the world and no matter what happens from here i don’t think that’s something that can ever be erased
